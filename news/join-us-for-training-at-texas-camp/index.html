Join us for training at Texas Camp

Published
March 26, 2018

Want to learn to do it better with Drupal? Come on down to Texas Camp on Thursday, May 31, to join us for one of our excellent training opportunities. Training ain’t included in the price of general admission to Texas Camp, but for $25, you can enjoy a full-day of learning from one of our expert trainers.

The training opportunities we have lined up are:

  • Drupal 8 Certification Crash Course
  • Actionable Strategies to Improve Visitor Experience
  • Visual Regression Testing with BackstopJS

This is a great opportunity for you or members of your team to get hands-on experience with new material. Make sure you purchase your ticket soon; training usually sells out well in advance of the camp.

If you’ve got a great idea that you think other Texas Drupalers would want to learn about, let us know. We’re open to adding more training options if we hear from y’all soon.
