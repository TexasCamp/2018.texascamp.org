Submit a Session

Texas Camp is soliciting sessions with all levels of Drupal users in mind. If you're passionate about the topic, we want to hear it! Subject matter can be Drupal, PHP in general, related technologies, soft skills, testing, tools, workflow, etc.

Sessions should be 45 minutes long to fit in a 60-minute time slot -- with an additional 10 minutes for audience Q+A. You’re welcome to promote a project or organization you are working on if it is relevant, but please no sales pitches.

Speakers are expected to follow our Code of Conduct.

Speakers receive one free ticket to Texas Camp (training not included) and access to the infamous Speaker & Volunteer dinner on Thursday, May 31st. If you buy a ticket and your session is selected, you can gift your ticket or receive a refund. 

Session submissions for 2018 have closed. You can view submitted sessions or buy your ticket for the event.
